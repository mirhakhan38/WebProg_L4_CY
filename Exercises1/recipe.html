BROWNIES

A brownie a day keeps the frownies away

DESCRIPTION

Brownies are chocolaty baked products made in square or rectangle shapes. They are commonly consumed around the world and are made with flour, fat (oil or butter), sugar, eggs and chocolate (molten or cocoa powder).

INGREDIENTS

  • 115g unsalted melted butter
  • 30g Cocoa powder
  • 100g Caster sugar
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp coffee
  • 100g flour
  • 20g bread flour
  • 1/2tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt

METHOD

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C and line the pan with parchement paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Crack in eggs and beat them one at a time, and then add vanila extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl whisk together flour, bread flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry Ingredientsin the wet Ingredients and mix.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips into the mixture.
  7. Add the mixture into the pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes.

TOPPINGS FOR YOUR BROWNIES

  • Add a layer of caramel to the top to add a sweet taste.
  • Coat your brownies with a raspberry swirl for an elegant touch.
  • You can add slices or whole fresh strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries on top of your brownies.
  • You can Sprinkle some grounded coffee or flaky salt.
