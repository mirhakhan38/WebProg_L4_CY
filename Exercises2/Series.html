PLOT

Strangers from Hell is a South Korean psychological thriller drama series based on a popular webtoon. It follows the story of Yoon Jong-woo, a young man who moves to Seoul to start a new life. He rents a cheap, rundown apartment in a mysterious building, only to find that his neighbors are strange, unsettling, and have a lot of dark secrets. As the story unfolds, Jong-woo becomes trapped in a web of paranoia and fear, with his life spiraling out of control as he tries to understand the truth about his eerie neighbors.
